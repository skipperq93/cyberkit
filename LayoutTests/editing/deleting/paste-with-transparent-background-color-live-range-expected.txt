This tests cutting and pasting a content with inline "background: transparent;" into a document with a style rule that overrides this style.
CyberKit should preserve the inline style declaration. To manually test, cut and paste "hello world CyberKit" below. "world" should not be highlighted in blue.

Before cut and paste:
| "<#selection-anchor>hello "
| <span>
|   class="test"
|   style="background: transparent;"
|   "world"
| " CyberKit<#selection-focus>"

After cut and paste:
| "hello "
| <span>
|   class="test"
|   style="background-color: transparent;"
|   "world"
| " CyberKit<#selection-caret>"
| <br>
